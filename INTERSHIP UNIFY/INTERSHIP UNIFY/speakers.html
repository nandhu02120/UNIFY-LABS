Keynote Speakers

Meet the brilliant minds who will be speaking at DevConf 2026.

Sarah Connor

Chief AI Scientist at CyberDyne Systems

Sarah is a pioneer in neural networks and machine learning. She has led groundbreaking research in predictive algorithms and autonomous systems.

At DevConf, she will be discussing the ethical implications of AGI and the future of human-AI collaboration.

Specializations:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Robotics
  • Ethics in Tech

Speaker Introduction Video:

Dr. Alan Grant

Senior Data Analyst & Paleontologist

Alan specializes in analyzing complex biological data sets. Recently he has pivoted to data science, applying his knowledge of chaotic systems to big data problems.

He will share insights on handling unpredictable data streams and legacy codebases.

Specializations:

  • Data Science
  • Chaos Theory
  • Python

Ellen Ripley

DevOps Lead at Weyland-Yutani

Ellen is an expert in crisis management and system reliability. She has experience managing mission-critical infrastructure in hostile environments.

Her talk will cover "Surviving Production Outages" and building resilient systems.

Specializations:

  • Site Reliability Engineering
  • Cloud Infrastructure
  • Crisis Management

Speaker Introduction Video:

Thomas "Neo" Anderson

Lead Software Architect at MetaCortex

Thomas is a legendary hacker turned software architect. He is known for seeing the code behind the reality and finding exploits in secure systems.

He will be demonstrating advanced cybersecurity techniques and how to escape the simulation of bad code.

Specializations:

  • Cybersecurity
  • Virtual Reality
  • System Architecture

Speaker Introduction Video:

Dana Scully

Forensic Data Investigator

Dana brings a scientific and skeptical approach to debugging. Her rigorous methodology has solved some of the most mysterious bugs in the industry.

Join her session on "The Truth is in the Logs: Advanced Debugging Techniques."

Specializations:

  • Forensics
  • Debugging
  • C++ / Systems Programming

Marty McFly

Full Stack Developer at Hill Valley Tech

Marty is a young developer with a unique perspective on legacy code. He specializes in modernizing old applications and time-sensitive data processing.

His keynote "Back to the Future of JavaScript" will explore the evolution of web frameworks.

Specializations:

  • JavaScript / TypeScript
  • Web Performance
  • Legacy Migration
