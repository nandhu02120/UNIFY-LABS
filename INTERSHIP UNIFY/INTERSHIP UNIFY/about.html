About DevConf 2026

DevConf 2026 is dedicated to fostering a community of developers who are passionate about building the future. Our mission is to provide an inclusive platform for learning, sharing, and networking.

Founded in 2015, DevConf has grown from a small local meetup to a major international event. We have hosted thousands of attendees from over 50 countries, featuring world-class speakers and cutting-edge topics.

This year, we are focusing on "The Next Era of Code," exploring how AI, quantum computing, and decentralized web technologies are reshaping the landscape of software development.

Why Attend?

  • Learn from industry experts and thought leaders.
  • Gain hands-on experience in our interactive workshops.
  • Network with fellow developers and potential employers.
  • Discover the latest tools and frameworks in the tech world.
  • Get inspired by success stories and innovative projects.

Who Should Attend?

  1. Software Engineers and Developers
  2. Data Scientists and AI Researchers
  3. Product Managers and UX Designers
  4. Tech Students and Bootcamp Grads
  5. CTOs and Tech Executives
