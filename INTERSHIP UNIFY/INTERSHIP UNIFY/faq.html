General Questions

When is DevConf 2026 taking place?

Where is the conference located?
The conference will take place at the Tech Convention Center in Innovation City. You can find the exact address and directions on our Contact page.
Can I attend both days or just one day?
Yes! You can choose to attend both days or just one day. Different registration packages are available to suit your needs. Check our registration page for details.
Is DevConf 2026 online or in-person?
DevConf 2026 is an in-person event. However, we may offer selected sessions through live streaming. Please contact us for more information about streaming options.
What's the theme of this year's conference?
This year's theme is "The Next Era of Code," focusing on how AI, quantum computing, and decentralized web technologies are reshaping software development.

Registration & Tickets

How do I register for DevConf 2026?
Simply visit our registration page and fill out the form with your personal and professional information. You'll receive a confirmation email once your registration is complete.
What are the different registration types available?
We offer three registration packages: Standard Pass ($99), VIP Pass ($199), and Student Pass ($29). Each package includes different levels of access and perks.
Is there a student discount?
Yes! Students can register for just $29 with a valid student ID. The Student Pass includes full access to all sessions and workshops.
Can I get a refund if I can't attend?
We offer full refunds for cancellations made up to 30 days before the event. Cancellations made after that date are non-refundable. Please contact us for more details.
What's included in the VIP Pass?
The VIP Pass includes: priority seating, exclusive VIP lounge access, special networking breakfast, free merchandise, and a personalized speaker meet-and-greet opportunity.
Is group registration available?
Yes! Groups of 10 or more can receive special discounts. Please email our team at info@devconf2026.com for group pricing.

Conference Content & Schedule

Who are the keynote speakers?
We have an amazing lineup of six keynote speakers including Sarah Connor (AI Expert), Dr. Alan Grant (Data Science), Ellen Ripley (DevOps), Neo Anderson (Cybersecurity), Dana Scully (Debugging), and Marty McFly (Web Development). View full bios on our Speakers page.
How many sessions will be offered each day?
We'll have approximately 15-20 sessions per day across multiple tracks, including keynotes, workshops, and breakout sessions. The full schedule is available on our Schedule page.
What conference tracks are available?
This year we're covering: Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Web Development, Cloud & DevOps, Cybersecurity, Mobile Development, and Data Science.
Can I attend workshops without registering for the full conference?
No, workshops are only available to registered conference attendees. However, workshop access is included with all registration packages.
Are slides and recordings available after the conference?
Yes! After the conference, registered attendees will receive access to session recordings and speaker slides. These will be available for 6 months following the event.
Can I suggest topics for next year's conference?
Absolutely! We welcome suggestions. Please send your topic ideas to speakers@devconf2026.com.

Logistics & Support

What should I bring to the conference?
Bring your laptop, charger, business cards, and a notebook. We'll provide name badges, a conference guide, and lunch. Parking information will be sent to all registered attendees.
Is there free WiFi at the venue?
Yes! High-speed WiFi will be available throughout the convention center for all attendees. Connection details will be provided on your badge.
Are meals provided during the conference?
Lunch is included all attendees on both days. We'll offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please note dietary restrictions during registration.
Is parking available at the venue?
Yes, free parking is available for all registered attendees. A parking pass will be included in your registration confirmation email.
Are childcare services available?
We do not have on-site childcare facilities. Please contact us if you have special requirements or questions regarding bringing family members.
Is the venue accessible for people with disabilities?
Yes, the Tech Convention Center is fully ADA compliant with wheelchair accessibility, accessible restrooms, and accommodations available. Please notify us of specific accessibility needs during registration.
What's the cancellation policy?
We have a 30-day cancellation policy. Cancellations made 30+ days before the event receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 30 days are non-refundable. No refunds for no-shows.
How can I update my registration information?
You can update your registration details by visiting the registration page or emailing us at info@devconf2026.com.

Sponsorship Opportunities

How can my company become a sponsor?
We offer multiple sponsorship tiers with different benefits and visibility levels. Contact our sponsorship team at sponsors@devconf2026.com for more information.
What are the sponsorship benefits?
Benefits vary by tier and include: logo placement on the website, keynote mentions, booth space, free tickets, and exclusive networking opportunities. See details on our Sponsors page.
What's the difference between the sponsorship tiers?
We offer Platinum, Gold, and Silver sponsorship levels. Platinum sponsors get the most visibility with keynote mentions and large booth space. Silver sponsors have shared booth space and website listing. View full details on our Sponsors page.
Are speaking opportunities available for sponsors?
Yes! Platinum sponsors can feature a speaker in one of our sessions. Contact our team for more information about speaking opportunities.
